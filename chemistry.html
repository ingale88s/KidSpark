2 States of Matter ❄️💧💨

Everything around you is made of tiny particles called molecules! How fast they move depends on temperature. Watch what happens to water molecules as you heat or cool them!

🌡️
20°C
💧 Liquid
At 20°C, water is a liquid. The molecules move freely but stay close together.

🧊💧💨 The Three States

Solid (ICE): molecules barely move, locked in place. Liquid (WATER): molecules flow around freely. Gas (STEAM): molecules fly everywhere super fast! The same H₂O becomes all three depending on temperature!

🔬 pH Scale

pH 0-6 = Acid (lemon juice is pH 2!). pH 7 = Neutral (pure water). pH 8-14 = Base (baking soda is pH 9). Scientists use special paper called litmus paper that changes color to test pH!
