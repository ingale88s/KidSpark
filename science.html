3 Day & Night 🌍☀️

Why do we have day and night? The Earth spins around once every 24 hours! When your side faces the Sun, it's daytime. When it faces away, it's night! Spin the Earth and answer the quiz!

🔭 Cool Fact!

Earth takes exactly 24 hours to spin once — that's why a day is 24 hours long! At the same time, Earth is also orbiting around the Sun, which takes 365 days — that's one year!
